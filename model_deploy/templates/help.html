How to Use the Mask Detector Model

Download any image from the internet or use a picture of yourself or friends that is saved on your computer. Click the 'Choose Image' button on the Predict page and then navigate to the image saved on your computer that you want to see if the person in the image is wearing a mask. Click 'Predict' and then the model will print the percentages that the person in your image is wearing a mask or not!
