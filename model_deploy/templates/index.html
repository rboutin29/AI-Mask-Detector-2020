Why Wear a Mask?

Masks help limit the spread of highly contagious viruses such as Covid-19 that can be transmitted via air particles. Not only is it recommned to wear a mask; it is also recommned that people social distance. Socially distance entails avoiding large gatherings of people.

How the Model was Developed

The model was developed using Tensorflow. Since this model involves predicting aspects in an image, a convolutional neural network was designed, built, trained, and then tested. The tests resulted in a 94% accuracy predicting a person in the image has a mask on and a 100% accuracy in predicting an individual was not wearing a mask or not wearing a mask properly.
